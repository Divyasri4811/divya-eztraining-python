#dictionary

dictionary contains element 2 units keys and values.
keys must be unique.
{1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 7}
d={1:"one",2:"two"}
         
d
         
{1: 'one', 2: 'two'}
type(d)
         
<class 'dict'>
d.keys()
         
dict_keys([1, 2])
d.values()
         
dict_values(['one', 'two'])
d.items()
         
dict_items([(1, 'one'), (2, 'two')])
